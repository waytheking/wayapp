About Us

BINGKAS (Bantuan Kehidupan Sejahtera Selangor) is an initiative introduced by the Selangor State Government in the Budget presented on 26 November 2021.

Recognizing the need to enhance the financial resilience of families in Selangor, especially post-COVID-19, BINGKAS provides RM300 monthly to 30,000 families, aiming to alleviate economic challenges.

This initiative helps ease financial burdens, enabling family heads to purchase essential items, including educational materials, thereby improving the quality of life for Selangor citizens.

FAQ

BINGKAS (Bantuan Kehidupan Sejahtera Selangor) is an initiative by the Selangor State Government providing RM300 monthly for basic necessities to families in need.

You can register for BINGKAS by visiting our registration page and following the provided instructions. Ensure you have all necessary documents ready for verification.

  • Must be a Malaysian citizen.
  • Must be born in or have settled in Selangor for more than 15 years.
  • Registered voters in Selangor.
  • Families with dependents aged 18 and under.
  • Household income below RM3000.

Once approved for BINGKAS, financial assistance will be credited to your registered bank account monthly. Ensure your bank details are accurate during registration.

You can contact BINGKAS support through our official website contact form, via email at support@bingkas.my, or by calling our helpline at 1-800-BINGKAS.
